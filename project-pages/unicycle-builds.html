Unicycle/Bike Builds

Over the years, I have built and maintained many wheeled vehicles. Here are some of them.

27.5" Mountain Unicycle

This is my pride and joy. It has a carbon seat base and rim, hydraulic disc brakes, and a custom welded pink handle for extra control of the unicycle while braking.

36" Touring Unicycle

This unicycle has a much larger wheel for riding longer distances. It has handlebars for taking some weight off from my butt and onto my hands, as well as racks that I strap supplies onto. I use it primarily for touring, this photo is from a 350 mile ride I did down the California coast.

20" Trials Unicycle

This unicycle has a small wheel for extra control. I use it to ride trials, which is where one jumps around on obstacles, rides on rails, etc. It has a seat that is just a bar to give myself a better place to grip onto, as well as save some weight.

Road Bike

This road bike I've had since I was 14. It has simple parts and has been very reliable. I took it apart to repaint once and have done various maintenence projects on it over the years.

Trials Bike

This is my trials mod bike. I don't ride it much but it was fun to put together.
