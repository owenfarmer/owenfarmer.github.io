Air Rebreather

This device uses the heat from the air the user breathes out to heat up the air the user breathes in.

Air Rebreather

Design

This device attaches to a pre-existing mask base. It has a stack of thin sheet metal sheets, with a small gap between each sheet. The gaps between sheets create air channels and the warm exit air would heat up the sheets, which would in turn heat up the cold entrance air.

First, to get the shape of the existing mask in CAD, we scanned the surfaces of the key locating surfaces using a ROMER Arm.

With the location of the entrance/exit holes and the nose piece in CAD, we designed the housing for the metal sheets.
