North American Unicycle Championship Wins

Every year there is a North American Unicycling Championships somewhere in the United States. I have won the trials event 3 times, and have various other podium finishes and age group wins as well. I've also attended the 2022 Unicycle World Championships in France, the European Unicycle Championships in 2017 in The Netherlands, and the North American Unicycle Championships in 2015 (Wisconsin) and 2017 (Washington).

Speed Trials Champion 2016

Speed Trials is an event where a course is set up with various obstacles that the riders must clear. The rider who makes their way through the fastest without touching the floor or dismounting wins. This year the competition was hosted in South Dakota.

Trials Champion 2022

In the trials event, a course with 60 obstacles is set up and the rider who completes the most lines in 2 hours wins. Obstacles this year included skis, tires, playground items, and a trailer bed. In 2022 the championships were hosted in Ohio.

Trials Champion 2023

In addition to winning this year I planned the event, and designed and set the course as well. This year's competition took place in Minnesota.

UCI National Bike Trials 2023

I competed in the Bike Trials competition in Bentonville Arkansas and won the mountain bike division.
